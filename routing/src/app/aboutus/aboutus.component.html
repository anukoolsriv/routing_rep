Larsen & Toubro Infotech Limited (LTI) is a global IT solutions and services company based in Mumbai, India. LTI was ranked number 6 [4] in India IT companies in 2013–2014. The company has 39 offices in 30 countries.[5] It employs standards of the Software Engineering Institute's (SEI) Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI) and is a Maturity Level 5 assessed organisation.[6]
